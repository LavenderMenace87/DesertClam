On Neurodivergence

Several members of Desert Clam are neurodivergent. We name this not as a liability but as a design feature. Neurodivergent minds built most of the interesting things in human history — because they notice what others overlook, make connections others miss, and refuse to accept that the way things are is the way things must be. A community of neurodivergent people growing their own food and building their own systems is not a risk. It is what happens when the people who think differently finally have a container that fits them.
